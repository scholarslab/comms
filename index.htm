Scholars' Lab Communications Information

In the case that external service providers (Slack, Zoom) and University services (email, NetBadge) go down, but Github (where this page is hosted) does not, this is a page to help coordinate communications. Currently, only primary channels are listed, but watch this space if services start to go down.

Text/Asynchronous Messaging

  1. Scholars' Lab Slack (primary)

AV/Synchronous Messaging

  1. UVA Zoom (primary)

Direct contact

Contact information for all staff is listed on a pinned link in the staff-only Slack channel. We'll re-evaluate how to distribute this information if Slack goes down.

Communication failure coordination

In case of major service disruption, we can meet on the WebRTC service Whereby to discuss how to get back online. Currently, the Scholarslab channel there is on a free plan with a limit of 4 participants, so this should (for now) probably remain reserved for getting alternative channels up.
